\textbf{Question 3.} Give the negation of
   \[ \forall q< 0\ \exists r > 7\ \forall t > 3, \ |t^2 - q^3| > r.\]

\textbf{Solution.} The negation of the statement is
  \[ \forall q > 0\ \exists r < 7\ \forall t \in (2,3), \ |t^2 - q^3| > r. \]
